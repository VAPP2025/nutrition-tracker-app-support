Nutrition Tracker — Support

Welcome! This page provides basic support information for the Nutrition Tracker app.

About the App

Nutrition Tracker helps users build healthier daily habits by making nutrition and wellness tracking simple and convenient. You can log meals, water intake, and medicine, choose a diet preference, and enable reminders for important times.

Key Features

  • Log Meals — Track breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.
  • Log Water — Record water intake and view daily totals.
  • Log Medicine — Note when doses are taken for personal tracking.
  • Choose Diet — Select from common diet styles (balanced, low-carb, high-protein, vegetarian, etc.).
  • Reminders — Optional notifications for meals, water, and medicine.
  • Simple Dashboard — See your daily logs in one place.

Troubleshooting

If you experience issues, try these steps:

  1. Restart the app.
  2. Ensure your device iOS is up to date.
  3. Confirm notification permissions in Settings if reminders are not appearing.
  4. Check your internet connection if using iCloud sync.

Privacy

Nutrition Tracker stores logs locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud sync, selected data may be stored in your iCloud account. We do not sell or share personal data to third parties.
